About Sychlopedia Chemicals

Who We Are

Established in 2025, Sychlopedia Chemicals is a premier supplier of high-quality laboratory and industrial chemicals across India. Inspired by industry leaders like Siddhi Chemicals, we focus on purity, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide reliable, safe, and competitively priced chemicals, ensuring timely delivery and superior service.

Vision: To be a trusted partner in the chemical supply chain, promoting innovation, quality, and sustainable practices.

Quality Assurance

We adhere strictly to international standards, conducting rigorous testing to ensure purity and consistency in everything we supply.

Safety First

Our top priority is safety—whether it's handling, packaging, or transporting chemicals, we follow strict safety protocols.

Customer Focus

We build lasting relationships through transparency, responsive service, and customized solutions tailored to client needs.

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly practices and minimizing environmental impact across our operations.
